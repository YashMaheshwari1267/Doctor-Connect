This is a free healthcare webapp that helps you access good quality medical service at your fingertips. It a one-step solution to your various problems like finding doctors for specific disease or problem in your city and book appointment just with a click. Moreover, doctor can track the health history of his patient he is going to examine. The users can also keep their all reports and medication history in their profile so they not need to carry each and everything to the doctor and also pay for doctor's fee online.
